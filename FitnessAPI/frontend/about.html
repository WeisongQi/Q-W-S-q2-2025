About FitnessAPI

Here’s how to use the app to stay fit and motivated:

Start a Workout

  • Click "Start Workout"
  • The app will randomly select 5 exercises
  • Follow the instructions and complete the reps — timer included!

Add a New Exercise

  • Click "Add new Exercise"
  • Fill in the exercise details (name, category, duration, etc.)
  • If you don’t provide an image, a default one will be used
  • Click "Add Exercise" to save it

View All Exercises

  • Click "Show all Exercise"
  • A full list of exercises will be displayed
  • You’ll see name, reps, duration, level, image, and more

Edit an Exercise

  • In the exercise list, click the ✏️ "Edit" button
  • The form will be auto-filled with that exercise’s info
  • Make your changes, then click "Confirm"

Tips

  • No login or signup required
  • Your data is stored locally
  • Train as often as you like — anytime, anywhere
